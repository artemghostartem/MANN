About Us

MANN Barbershop is an exclusively male story. We specialize in men's haircuts and shaving.

Our masters are professionals who constantly improve their skills and use only the best tools and professional cosmetics in their work.

Today MANN Barbershop is two spaces that are located in different parts of Kharkov and have their own individual characteristics and atmosphere, but at the same time, the quality of haircuts remains unchanged.
